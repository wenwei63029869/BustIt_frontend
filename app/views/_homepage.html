Find Out The Spy

Perfect for 7 players plus 1 judge and tons of audience.

  • Rules

Rules are simple

Citizens try to find out the spy, while spy tries stay until the last round.

Get your word

One play will get a special word that is slightly different from the one the rest get(e.g. Accounting and Finance). Each round each player will give one sentence of description about his/her word. The spy should try to guess what other people's word and don't be so obvious that everybody knows right away that you are the spy. PS: you don't want to be so specific that the spy can figure out what your word is or too broad that people will think you are a spy.

Vote

After each person gave their desription, they get to vote on whom they think is the spy. The one getting the most votes will be out. If the spy gets voted out, game is over. If not, next round starts. Then the rest vote on them again. This continues until there are only two people left, which means spy wins. PS: if more than one person get the most and equal amount of votes, they need to give a new description again.

Budge

Your win/loss will define your badge that show how good that you play this game. Now let's see where we are gonna play in a room.
